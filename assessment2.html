Sprint Updates
(click charts to enlarge them)

Week One (19/04 - 25/04)

During the first week, our team focused on getting to know the layout and structure of the project we took over. We conducted a thorough review of the product brief provided by stakeholders. We the discussed and decided upon the roles everyone would undertake during this part of the process.

Week Two (26/04 - 02/05)

By the end of the second week, we had fully understood the new requirements set out before us. We also had a good idea of how the code was structured and understood the architecture. This meant we were able to start the implementation of the new requirements along side our continuous integration. Work on the website also began, with it being split into assessment 1 and assessment 2.

Week Three (03/05 - 09/05)

At the end of week three, significant progress was made in completing documentation. All areas of the project had seen big progress in the writing up, and also in this week we started to create our tasks for our users to evaluate the game. The change log was also kept up to date with all the changes made to documents.

Week Four (10/05 - 16/05)

In week four, we had finished most of the documents, with the main focus being on finishing the implementation and testing. Also, during this week we completed our user evaluation, and wrote up all the responses and feedback in the document. This lead to a few tweaks being required in the implementation that we tried to get sorted as quick as possible.

Week Five (17/05 - 23/05)

In this last week, we finished up all the documents to have all the required information in them. we then just had our last push to get all the implementation and testing finished in time for the deadline. This was the main focus of the week, and the final thing that was done was converting all the documents to pdfs and uploading them to the website for easy access.
